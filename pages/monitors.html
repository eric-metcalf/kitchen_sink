Bay Area In-Store Pickup
Eight of ten can be picked up locally.
Retailer badges marked with a ◆ indicate Bay Area physical stores where you can see or collect the monitor today. Three local retailers cover almost everything on this list:

Best Buy (multiple Bay Area locations including Cupertino, Sunnyvale, Mountain View, San Jose) carries the Dell P3225QE, U3225QE, BenQ PD3225U, Samsung S80UD, and LG 32UN880. Best for the new Dell UltraSharp/Pro and Samsung models — call ahead since stock varies by store.

Costco (Santa Clara, Sunnyvale, Mountain View, etc., membership required) carries the Dell S3225QC QD-OLED, Dell U3223QZ video conferencing, and LG 32UN880 ErgoStand. Online prices for the U3223QZ are warehouse-only — phone the store for exact pricing.

Micro Center Santa Clara (5201 Stevens Creek Blvd) carries the BenQ PD3220U and Dell S3225QC. Member pricing requires free account sign-in.

The two with no Bay Area pickup are the ASUS ProArt PA329CRV and BenQ PD3226G — both are online-order only (ASUS, B&H, Newegg, Amazon for the ProArt; BenQ, B&H, or Amazon for the PD3226G).
